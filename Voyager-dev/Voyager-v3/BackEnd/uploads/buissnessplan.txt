



FILE # 1




Business Name: EcoClean Solutions
Location: San Francisco, California
Legal Structure: Limited Liability Company (LLC)
Description: EcoClean Solutions provides eco-friendly cleaning products and services for residential and commercial spaces.
Mission: To promote sustainability and environmental consciousness by offering effective cleaning solutions that minimize harm to the planet.
Problem Solved: Harmful chemical exposure and environmental pollution caused by traditional cleaning products.
Result Created: Provides customers with a clean and healthy environment while reducing their carbon footprint.
Five-Year Goals: Expand geographical reach, innovate new eco-friendly cleaning technologies, and become a recognized global leader in sustainable cleaning solutions.
